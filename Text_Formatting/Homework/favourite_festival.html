Diwali is one of the most popular festivals in India. It is also called the Festival of Lights. People decorate their houses with diyas, candles, and colorful lights.

How We Celebrate

  1. Clean and decorate the house.
  2. Light diyas and candles in the evening.
  3. Exchange gifts and sweets with friends and family.

What I Love About Diwali

  • The beautiful lights and decorations everywhere.
  • Delicious sweets like laddoos and barfi.
  • Spending time with my family and friends.

Conclusion: Diwali teaches us the value of goodness and light over darkness and evil.
